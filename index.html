Horoscope 2021 (Main goal is self improvement, motivation, and positive astrology) Hello good people, welcome to vedic nakshatra wisdom -the arena of positive astrology. The major transit that is happenning on 2021 is the transit of Jupiter. Jupiter transits to Kumbha on April 05, 2021, retrogrates to Makara on Sep 14 2021, and then directs back again to Kumbha on Nov 20, 2021. Saturn, Rahu and Ketu will not change rashi's on 2021, and will remain on capricorn, scorpio and Taurus respectively. For fast moving planets, the Sun stays in each sign for approximately 30 days.The Moon changes signs every 2 ½ days, Mercury can be in a sign for 15- 60 days. Venus stays in a sign from 23 days to 2 months depending on the sign, Mars changes signs about every 2 months. So impacts of slow moving planets will be considered for 2021 horoscope prediction, unless small planet plays a major role. Ashtakavarga point plays a significant role on result of planet transit on each sign, so higher the ashtakavarga point better the planets results will be, you can get your ashtakavarga points for free online. This horoscope is based on Ascendant sign. Aries For Aries people Jupiter will be on your tenth house for half of the year and will be on your eleventh house for rest of the year. Saturn will be on your tenth house for the entire year, rahu will be on 2nd and ketu will be on your 8th house. This year the major concentration of your life would be on social status, comfort, finance, career, savings and transformation. Since the Saturn which represents hard work dedication, reality, common people, etc resides on your tenth house with the guru the planet of wisdom for first half, your chances of getting a recognisation increases if you had worked hard for your goals, since saturn is willing to give you the positive result if you have dedication, persistence and willingness to work hard to achieve what you desire. Letting down of your ego and working with every kind of people with equality is your key to achieve what your desire this year. Since planet Jupiter will be moving to your 11th house on late November your hard work and dedication have higher chances to frucity after November. With rahu on 2nd house you might be tempted on spending some of your saved money on luxuries, but remember to spend with caution, since ketu is looking at your rahu and waiting to see when your rahu kicks in and you spend that hard money for something not so important, and ketu can transform you, and make realize the imporatance of that saving. So be careful on your spending, and spend cautiously, and spending only what is necessary for benefit of the whole family. Ketu might give you some interest on things that are not of material nature, so meditation seems to be a good option for your to start this year, if you have not done so. Trust me solution to every problem is within you and you will definitiky relize that after some good practise of meditation, other it will just be another clicied statement. On contrary rahu can give you some foreign wealth, if you have some investement outside of your country, or is working for foreign company. Rahu invokes foreign related stuff, so if you working with foreign peple or have invested on foreign money, there is a chances of a gain.
